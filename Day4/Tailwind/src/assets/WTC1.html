13th Episode – Discovery Series: Understanding the Business Environment in Spain
Add to Calendar 2026-07-07 15:00 2026-07-07 16:30 13th Episode – Discovery Series: Understanding the Business Environment in Spain

Join us for the 13th edition of the Discovery Series, a virtual platform designed to provide Indian entrepreneurs with practical insights into global markets.

This session focuses on Spain, exploring key aspects of doing business including market entry strategies, regulatory and partnership frameworks, emerging sectors and opportunities, and the business culture and lifestyle in the region.

Panelists:

  • Thomas Joseph, Managing Director, Mapp Law Chambers
  • Carles Anglada, CEO, World Trade Centre Barcelona
  • Berta Perez, International Director, Barcelona Chamber of Commerce
  • Krishnan Menon, Founder & CEO, The Spanish Alchemist
  • Karnvir Mundrey, Podcaster & Founder and Chief Ideation Officer, Atharva Marcom (Moderator)

 

This episode is organized in association with the Bangalore Chamber of Industry and Commerce (BCIC) and World Trade Centre Barcelona.
